Mushroom Stir-Fry

Description

This quick and easy mushroom stir-fry is perfect for a healthy weeknight meal. It's packed with flavor and can be customized with your favorite vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp cornstarch

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat.
  2. Add garlic and ginger, stir for 30 seconds.
  3. Add mushrooms and stir-fry for 5-7 minutes until tender.
  4. Mix soy sauce and cornstarch, add to the pan.
  5. Cook for another minute until sauce thickens.
