Zucchini Rollatini

Description

This zucchini rollatini recipe is a healthy twist on traditional lasagna. The zucchini is rolled up with a flavorful filling and baked to perfection.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium zucchini, halved lengthwise
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cut zucchini lengthwise and scoop out the centers.
  3. Mix ricotta, mozzarella, parmesan, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Fill each zucchini half with the cheese mixture.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes until golden brown.
