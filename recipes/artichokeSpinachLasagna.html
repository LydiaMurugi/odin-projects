Artichoke Spinach Lasagna

Description

This lasagna combines the earthy flavors of artichokes and spinach in a rich, creamy sauce layered with pasta sheets.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1 can (14 oz) artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
  • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

Steps

  1. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions.
  2. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  3. In a large bowl, mix ricotta, mozzarella, parmesan, artichokes, spinach, and garlic.
  4. Spread a thin layer of sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  5. Layer noodles, filling, and sauce in alternating layers.
  6. Bake for 30 minutes or until bubbly and golden brown.
